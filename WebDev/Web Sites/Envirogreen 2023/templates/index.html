PROGRAMS

In 2020, EVEFI applied for government permits to offer the Senior High School and Bachelor of Agricultural Technology Programs from both the Department of Education (DepEd), and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). There are two tracks offered for the Senior High School Program namely the Academic and TVL tracks. For the Academic track, two strands are offered, the Accounting, Business, and Management strand, and the Humanities and Social Sciences strand. For the TVL track, on the other hand, Agri-Fishery Arts, and Home Economics strands. For the Agri Fishery Arts, Agricultural Crops Production NCII and Pest Management (Vegetables) NCII are offered while Local Guiding Services NCII and Tourism Management NCII are offered to students.
